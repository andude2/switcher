Enter: a lightweight Switcher window to target dannet connected peers and navigate between your box army windows with ease.

Left click on the name to bring that character's window to the foreground.  Right click on the name to target them (and /face!).  If you have an item on your cursor, right click on the name and open a trade window.

Choose to sort by name or class, 1 button to load the window in place across all your toons.

`/lua run switcher` to start.
